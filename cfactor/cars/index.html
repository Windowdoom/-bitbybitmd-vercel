⚡ THE SINGLE RULE THE WHOLE COURSE IS BUILT ON
The passage is the only authority.

For the duration of a passage, your outside knowledge does not count — the author wins. An answer that is true in the real world but contradicts the passage is wrong; an answer that is false in the real world but matches the passage is correct. Four years of science training built the opposite reflex, and it's exactly the reflex CARS punishes. The good news: it's a trainable switch, not a personality change.
