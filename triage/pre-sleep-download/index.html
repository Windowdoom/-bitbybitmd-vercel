Pre-sleep download.

Ten minutes. Four segments. Sleep is the highest-leverage intervention for retention. The day ends here.

Tonight
Phone face down. Lights low. Then begin.
Segment 1, brain dump3:00
Type every worry that is in your head right now. One per line. No editing. No grammar. No priority. Just dump.
Segment 2, today’s catch2:00
One thing you learned today. One. Not a list. Specific enough to teach to someone tomorrow.
Segment 3, tomorrow’s first action2:00
What is the single first thing you do tomorrow when you sit at the desk? Not the whole day. The first action. The opening move.
Segment 4, body scan3:00
